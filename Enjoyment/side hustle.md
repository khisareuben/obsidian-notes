
### 📚 Platforms for Selling Notes & PDFs

- **Gumroad**
    
    - Very popular for selling digital products (PDFs, e‑books, code snippets).
    - You set your own price (even $1).
    - Simple link you can share anywhere.
        
- **Ko‑fi**
    
    - Lets people “tip” you for your work.
    - You can upload your notes as a digital download.
    - Works well if you want small contributions.
        
- **Payhip**
    
    - Similar to Gumroad, focused on e‑books and PDFs.
    - Easy to set up, supports low‑cost items.
        
- **Etsy (Digital Downloads)**
    
    - People sell study guides, planners, and notes.
    - More competitive, but has a big audience.
        
- **Self‑distribution**
    
    - You can also share your notes directly (Google Drive, Dropbox) and use **M‑Pesa or PayPal** for payments.
    - This is common in Kenya for small digital products.
        

### ✅ Practical Advice

- Start with **Gumroad or Payhip** — they’re simple, global, and allow small prices.
- Market your notes on **WhatsApp groups, Telegram, or LinkedIn** where students and beginners hang out.
- Even if you earn **$1 per download**, with enough reach you can hit your daily earning goal while studying.
